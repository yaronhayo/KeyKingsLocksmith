How We Started

Key Kings Locksmith opened in 2016 with one van and a straightforward goal: provide honest locksmith service to Anderson County residents and businesses.

Nine years later, we're still that same locally-owned operation—just with more experience, better equipment, and thousands of satisfied customers across Anderson, Greenville, Clemson, and surrounding areas.

We handle everything from emergency lockouts to complete commercial security installations. Some days we're helping a parent get back into their car at the grocery store. Other days we're installing master key systems for office buildings. Every situation gets the same professional attention.

We're not a franchise or a national chain with a local number. When you call Key Kings, you're reaching people who live and work in the same communities we serve. That accountability matters to us.
